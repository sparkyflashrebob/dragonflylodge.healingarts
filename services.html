Jin Shin Jyutsu

Jin Shin Jyutsu is a gentle Japanese healing art that harmonizes the life energy within our bodies. Utilizing 26 Safety Energy Locks (SELs) located along primary pathways, our practitioner applies soft finger pressure to release blocks that create physical, mental, and chronic strain.

During a JSJ session, you remain fully clothed, resting comfortably on a padded therapy table. Andrea uses gentle, concentrated listening touch on energy centers, matching your nervous system dynamics to promote a restorative state.

Therapeutic Touch

Therapeutic Touch (TT) is a modern, clinically tested healing modality based on natural energy alignment principles. By assessing the client's surrounding biofield, the practitioner works to soothe hyper-stimulated energy lines, helping the body channel its focus back toward self-repair.

TT is highly effective in hospital and clinical settings for accelerating wound healing, reducing chronic stress, and bringing intense emotional states back to a feeling of safety and calm.
