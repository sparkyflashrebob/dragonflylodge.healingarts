Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to a Jin Shin Jyutsu or Therapeutic Touch session?

For both modalities, you remain completely clothed. We recommend wearing loose, comfortable clothing (such as soft pants, cotton shirts, or activewear) that allows you to lie down and rest comfortably on the massage table.

How many sessions will I need to see results?

Many clients report immediate nervous system relief, localized pain reduction, or emotional clarity during their very first session. For chronic tension, fatigue, or recovery goals, we recommend a series of 3 to 5 weekly sessions to build a stable energetic baseline.

Can these healing modalities complement my standard medical treatment?

Yes. Both Jin Shin Jyutsu and Therapeutic Touch are non-invasive and function beautifully as integrative treatments alongside chemotherapy, physical rehabilitation, cardiovascular regimens, and psychological therapy. They support the body's natural restorative state but do not replace professional medical diagnosis.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations or schedule changes. Missed sessions or cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a 50% session fee.
