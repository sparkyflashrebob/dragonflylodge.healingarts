Meet Andrea

Andrea is the head, heart and hands of Dragonfly Lodge Healing Arts. The name of her practice derives from many years' experience enacting solo vision fasts in the desert East of the Sierras. These experiences represent a dynamic part of her personal healing journey, not a small part of which has been learning to live with grief and loss. There have also been many years of living with conditions (projects) of body, mind and spirit, which have led her on a path of healing. Along the way, it has also become vitally important to achieve and maintain sobriety. It's now been 27 years.

"I began my training in Therapeutic Touch in the mid-90's, and fell in with a wonderful group of Elders who took me beneath their wings. As a result of meeting them, I was also introduced to the healing art of Jin Shin Jyutsu. I dedicated myself to study, and soon became a Practitioner. I was thrilled to discover early on that I could practice not only with friends and family, but with our animal companions as well. I began giving treatments to dogs and horses, and they are, to this day, my favorite beings to work with!"

Core Principles

Deep Presence

We believe in the healing power of undivided attention. Every session begins with a grounding dialogue, allowing the practitioner to map the client's current energetic architecture and emotional state.

Subtle Energetics

Physical symptoms often start as blocks in our energy streams. By applying non-invasive touch, we help the body dissolve these static lines, restoring the natural, restorative flow of life-force energy.
