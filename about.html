Meet Andrea

Andrea is the heart and hands of Dragonfly Lodge. Committed to the path of integrative healing, she brings years of dedicated study and clinical experience in Jin Shin Jyutsu (JSJ) and Therapeutic Touch to every treatment session.

Her journey began as a search for solutions to personal fatigue and stress-induced disharmony. By discovering the power of light touch and biological alignment, Andrea realized the body possesses an incredible, self-regulating blueprint. She has since dedicated her practitioner career to holding space for others to discover their own equilibrium.

"Healing is not about fixing what is broken. It is a remembrance of what has always been whole."

Core Principles
